Growing a Garden

Hydratelyn output

Real-time soil moisture plot

I recently took an interest in soil science since a project at work may have applications in monitoring soil nutrients. This 3D printed pot I designed has a glass panel so you can see what's going on below the surface

My open-source system Hydratelyn is cycling the hyperspectral (405nm-750nm) light on a timer and monitoring moisture. I plan to add auto-watering and give it some personality with sassy alerts when it's dry like "Did you forget about me? I guess you don't care about me :("
